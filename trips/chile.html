San Pedro de Atacama

Days 6 – 11

The Atacama Desert

Mars on Earth · Endless Skies · Pure WOW

Fly Santiago → Calama, then transfer to San Pedro. Five full nights to slowly absorb what is genuinely one of the most extraordinary landscapes on Earth — salt flats, volcanoes, flamingos, geothermal fields, and the world's best stargazing.

Tierra Atacama Family Favorite ↗

Excellent guided experiences. The best family pick.

Explora Atacama Adventure-Luxe ↗

All-inclusive, expedition-style luxury.

Nayara Alto Atacama Relaxed ↗

Beautiful setting, more chilled vibe.
  • Valle de la LunaMoon Valley at sunset — top-3 moment of the trip.
  • Altiplanic LagoonsTurquoise lakes, flamingos, volcano scenery.
  • El Tatio GeysersPre-dawn drive into geothermal steam fields.
  • World-Class StargazingOne of the clearest night skies on the planet. Core family memory.
  • Flex Adventure DayHorseback, biking, salt flats, hot springs — or just the resort.
